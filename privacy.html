PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy matters. Here's how we handle your information.

OVERVIEW

JEAA Infosec LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at jeaainfosec.com.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service may collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

This data helps us improve our website and understand our audience. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Scheduling Information

When you schedule a consultation through our Google Calendar integration, your information is collected and processed directly by Google according to their Privacy Policy. We receive only the information you provide when booking (name, email, any notes you include).

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect personal information through forms on this website. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Information collected through our website is used to:

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve our services
  • Respond to consultation requests and inquiries
  • Provide the cybersecurity services you request
  • Comply with legal obligations

COOKIES

Our website uses cookies—small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — website analytics and traffic analysis
  • Google Calendar — consultation scheduling
  • Google Fonts — web typography

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies for details on how they handle your data.

DATA SECURITY

As a cybersecurity firm, we take data protection seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please schedule a consultation or reach out through our website.
